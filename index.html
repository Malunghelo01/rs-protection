About RS-Protection

RS Protection (Pty) Ltd was established in 2019 and is based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We are a 100% Black-Owned company and a Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor.

We provide professional security services to businesses, organisations, residential properties, commercial facilities, and individuals. Our mission is to protect people, assets, and property with integrity, professionalism, and reliability.
